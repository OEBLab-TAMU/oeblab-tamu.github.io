About the Lab

Welcome to OEB

The OEB Laboratory conducts both laboratory and field-based research studies to investigate musculoskeletal injury mechanisms across diverse work environments — computer work, VR/AR, professional vehicles, patient handling, and commercial fishing.

Now at Texas A&M School of Public Health, the OEB Lab continues advancing occupational health science through rigorous translational research.

Meet the Team

🦴 Musculoskeletal Injury Prevention

Investigating biomechanical mechanisms underlying work-related disorders across occupational settings.

📡 Bioinstrumentation & Wearables

Deploying motion capture, EMG, force measurement, and wearable sensors in real work environments.

🦾 Exoskeletons & Assistive Tech

Evaluating passive and active exoskeletons for reducing biomechanical load in high-risk industries.

🏭 Lab & Field Research

Bridging controlled lab studies and real-world field investigations to validate interventions.
